Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-08

24-0 F1 (“the Game”) is an unofficial fan project. This policy explains what data we may collect and why.

What we collect

  • Gameplay state is stored in your browser memory (and optionally localStorage) so a session can run. We do not require an account to play.
  • Analytics (if enabled): aggregate traffic such as page views, device type, and country via tools like Google Analytics or Vercel Analytics. Used to understand usage, not to sell personal profiles.
  • Advertising (if enabled): third parties such as Google AdSense may use cookies or similar technologies to show ads and measure performance. See Google’s policies for details.
  • Optional cloud features (if enabled later via Supabase): if you opt into leaderboards or saved campaigns, we store the data you submit (e.g. record, era, nickname) on our database provider.

Cookies, ads & consent (EEA / UK / Switzerland)

We use Google’s certified Consent Management Platform (CMP) via AdSense Privacy & messaging (Funding Choices) to request consent where required (European Economic Area, United Kingdom, and Switzerland).

Until you choose, advertising cookies are treated as denied under Google Consent Mode. After you select Consent, Do not consent, or options under Manage options, Google applies that choice to ad personalization and related storage. You can change your mind later via the privacy options Google provides on subsequent visits (where available).

Ads partners may set cookies for personalization, measurement, and fraud prevention only as allowed by your consent choices and applicable law. You can also control cookies in your browser settings.

Children

The Game is not directed at children under 13. Do not use the Game if you are under the age required by your jurisdiction for the services involved (including ads).

Third parties

  • Hosting / CDN (e.g. Vercel)
  • Fonts and static libraries loaded from public CDNs
  • Ad networks (e.g. Google AdSense) when ads are live
  • Database (e.g. Supabase) only if optional cloud features are turned on

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue on the project GitHub repository or contact the maintainer listed there.

Changes

We may update this policy as the Game evolves. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
